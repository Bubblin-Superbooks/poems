Perchance I, too, have rights, now veiled in darkness,— Rights, which the heavy drapery of the scaffold Now hides beneath its black and ample folds; Rights which, if my intent deceive me not, My sword shall one day rescue. To be brief:— I have received from churlish Fortune nothing But air, light, water,—Nature's general boon. Choose, then, between us two, for you must choose;— Say, will you wed the duke, or follow me?

 DONNA SOL. I'll follow you.
