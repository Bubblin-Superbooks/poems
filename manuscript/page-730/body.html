Milton's Appeal To Cromwell.

("Non! je n'y puis tenir.")

{CROMWELL, Act III. sc. iv.}

Stay! I no longer can contain myself, But cry you: Look on John, who bares his mind To Oliver—to Cromwell, Milton speaks! Despite a kindling eye and marvel deep A voice is lifted up without your leave; For I was never placed at council board To speak my promptings.
