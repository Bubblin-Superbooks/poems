As saint all worship thee; Thyself even hast the strong Thrill of divinity Mingled with thy small song.

Each grand old warrior Guards thee, submissive, proud; Mute thunders at thy door Sleep, that shall wake most loud.

Around thee foams the wild Bright sea, the lot of kings. Happier wert thou, my child, I' the woods a bird that sings!

NELSON R. TYERMAN.
