My Faenzetta has my heart! In Fieson� she The fairest! Nothing shall us part, Saving, in sooth, the Sea! And that not long! its rolling wave And such breeze holding now Will send me along to her I love— And so I made my bow.

We told thirty when we started From port so taut and fine, But thus our crew were parted, And now we number nine.
