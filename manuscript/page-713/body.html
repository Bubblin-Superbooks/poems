 DONNA SOL. And yet you promised?
 HERNANI _(falls on his knee.)_  Angel! in this hour,

Pursued by vengeance and oppressed by power— Even in this hour when death prepares to close In shame and pain a destiny of woes— Yes, I, who from the world proscribed and cast, Have nursed one dark remembrance of the past, E'en from my birth in sorrow's garment clad,
