("Avez-vous oui dire?")

{LES BURGRAVES, Part I., March, 1843.}

 JOB. Hast thou ne'er heard men say

That, in the Black Wood, 'twixt Cologne and Spire, Upon a rock flanked by the towering mountains, A castle stands, renowned among all castles? And in this fort, on piles of lava built, A burgrave dwells, among all burgraves famed?
