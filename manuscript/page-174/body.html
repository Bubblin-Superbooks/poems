Hark! a sound, Far and slight, Breathes around On the night High and higher, Nigh and nigher, Like a fire, Roaring, bright.
   Now, on 'tis sweeping
     With rattling beat,
   Like dwarf imp leaping
     In gallop fleet
   He flies, he prances,
   In frolic fancies,
   On wave-crest dances
     With pattering feet.
   Hark, the rising swell,
     With each new burst!
