Where your brood seven lie,
   Float in calm heavenly,
   Life passing evenly,

Waterfowl, waterfowl! often I dream For a rest Like your nest, Skirting the stream.

   Shine the sun tearfully
   Ere the clouds clear fully,
   Still you skim cheerfully,

Swallow, oh! swallow swift! often I sigh For a home Where you roam Nearing the sky!
