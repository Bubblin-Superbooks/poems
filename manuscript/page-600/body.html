They bore—each on himself—the insults laid On the dear foster-land: of naught afraid, Save of not finding foes enough to dare For Italy. Ah; gallant, free, and rare Young martyrs of a sacred cause,—Adieu! No more of life—no more of love—for you! No sweet long-straying in the star-lit glades At Ave-Mary, with the Italian maids; No welcome home!
