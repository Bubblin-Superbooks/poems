Oh, for a star to shine, a voice to sound— To raise some sudden note of music now Suited to night.

 HERN.           Capricious girl! your vow

Was poured for silence, and to be released From the thronged tumult of the marriage feast.

 DONNA SOL. Yes; but one bird to carol in the field,—

A nightingale, in mossy shade concealed,— A distant flute,—for music's stream can roll To soothe the heart, and harmonize the soul,— O! 'twould be bliss to listen.
