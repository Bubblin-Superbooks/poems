We're rovers of the restless main, But we've some conscience, mark! And we know what it is to reign, And finally did heark— Aye, masters of the narrow Neck, We hearkened to our heart, And gave him freedom on our deck, His town, and gold—in part.

My lucky mates for that were made Grandees of Old Castile, And maids of honor went to wed, Somewhere in sweet Seville;

Not they for me were fair enough, And so his Majesty Declared his daughter—'tis no scoff! My beauteous bride should be.
