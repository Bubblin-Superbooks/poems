gloom Of the winter-burdened cloud!— "But, in winter, most I bloom!"

Star of even! sunk the sun! Lost for e'er the ruddy line; And the earth is veiled in dun,— "Nay, in darkness, best I shine!"

O, my soul! art 'bove alarm, Quaffing thus the cup of gall— Canst thou face the grave with calm?— "Yes, the Christians smile at all."
