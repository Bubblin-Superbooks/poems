 Under the thickly-tangled branches, thus

Did I speak to him; he no answer gave.

 I shook my head, and moved myself away;

Then, from the copses, and from secret caves Hid in the wood, methought a ghostly voice Came forth and woke an echo in my souls As in the hollow of an amphora.
